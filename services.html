Our Services

Service One

Bridal Mehendi

Your wedding day deserves nothing less than perfection. Our bridal mehendi packages are meticulously designed to create a stunning visual narrative across your hands and feet, blending traditional motifs with personalized elements that tell your unique love story.

Each design is thoughtfully composed, incorporating intricate paisleys, floral networks, and symbolic imagery that reflect the richness of your heritage while embracing contemporary finesse.

We use only the finest natural henna, ensuring a rich, deep stain that lasts beautifully through your celebrations.

Service Two

Occasional Mehendi

Life's special moments deserve to be adorned. Whether it is a festival celebration, an engagement, a baby shower, or a festive gathering, our occasional mehendi designs bring warmth and elegance to every occasion.

We offer a range of styles from traditional Indian and Arabic patterns to lighter, more contemporary motifs. Each design is customized to match the tone and formality of your event, ensuring you feel beautiful and celebrated.

Our occasional packages are designed to be completed in a single session, making them perfect for busy schedules without compromising on artistry.

Service Three

Contemporary Designs

For the modern minimalist who appreciates subtlety and sophistication, our contemporary collection reimagines mehendi as a form of modern art. Clean lines, geometric patterns, negative space, and abstract motifs define this distinctive style.

Inspired by architecture, nature, and modern design principles, these creations offer a fresh perspective on henna artistry. They are particularly popular for editorial shoots, intimate ceremonies, and those who seek a unique, understated aesthetic.

Each contemporary design is a bespoke creation, developed in close consultation with you to ensure it reflects your personal style.
