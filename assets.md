## Image Sourcing Tips

- Use **Unsplash**, **Pexels**, or **Pixabay** for free high-quality stock photos
- For authentic mehendi images, consider **Shutterstock** or **Adobe Stock**
- Best search keywords: "henna hands", "mehendi ceremony", "henna artist", "bridal henna", "henna design close-up"
- Ensure all images have a **warm, golden, or natural light** tone to match the brand palette
- Avoid dark or overly saturated images
